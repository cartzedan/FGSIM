About Us

FULL GOSPEL SANCTUARY INT'L MINISTRIES

"Behold, I am the Lord, the GOD of all flesh, is there any thing too hard for me?" JEREMIAH 32 : 27

We are a citadel of God's presence, a ministry inspired and directed by the holy Spirit. Founded in the year 1997 by Pastor Andrew Folorunso with the word Jeremiah 32:27, nothing as ever been IMPOSSIBLE for God to do and never as He fail us. Our primary Mission is to

  • Make HEAVEN
  • TAKE as many PEOPLE as possible with US
  • DEPOPULATE the kingdom of HELL and POPULATE the kingdom of HEAVEN
